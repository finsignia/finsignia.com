Our Clients in Financial Services

Finsignia software engineers have previously worked with or at the following types of clients in the financial services sector:

  • Investment banks: specifically Morgan Stanley, BNP Paribas and Banc of America Securities.
  • Hedge funds: specifically Citadel Investment Group (Chicago), Stark Investments
  • Hedge fund ASP: specifically Sky Road ASP

Our software engineers have business analysis skills and experience with the following financial products:

  • Exchange Traded Derivatives (Options, Futures)
  • OTC Derivatives (Forwards, Swaps, OTC Options)
  • Credit products such as Asset Backed Securities (ABS), Mortgage Backed Securities (MBS), Collateralized Debt Obligations (CDO), Single name Credit Default Swaps (CDS), Credit Default Swap Index (CDSI)
  • Foreign Exchange (FX Spot, FX Options)
  • Capital Markets (Exchange Traded Common Stock, Private Equity, Corporate Bonds, Government Bonds, Munis)

Implementing the following types of systems:

  • Market data: historical prices, vendor symbols, tick data, spreads
  • Front office: asset-backed securities deal analysis
  • Middle office: security master tool (options, equities, futures), risk management
  • Back office: settlement, clearning, compliance
