Why would you hire us?

Hire us to solve complex problems in the high availability, big data and business intelligence problem spaces. Do not try to hire us to redesign your website!:)

We have been around the block a few times. This means we have seen a lot of recurring problems in our areas of expertise, what has worked and what hasn't. We also understand the financial services business domain much better than general purpose software consulting firms. This really does matter. It is one of the most underappreciated qualities of software engineers: domain knowledge.

Yet it can make or break the viability of a software product. Investing in smart partners who understand the nature of your domain, the direction your industry is heading in and are at the forefront of their own field of expertise pays off in the long run.
